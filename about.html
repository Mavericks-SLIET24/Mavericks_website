Who We Are

Team Mavericks is the official robotics club based at Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology (SLIET). It provides an ideal platform for students of Mechanical, Mechatronics, Electronics, Computers, and Electrical Engineering backgrounds to hone their skills and showcase their talent in many interdisciplinary activities.

Our Objectives

  • 1. To promote creativity and increase the technical know-how and productivity of all students at the Institute.
  • 2. To promote hands-on and co-operative learning, engage students in problem-solving, and work on Industrial Oriented Projects.
  • 3. To develop the spirit of entrepreneurship among the students of the Institute.
